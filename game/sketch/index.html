Here you'll find my design sketch/comp for the Studio 3 Game On Project.

For Studio 3, I am creating Alchemy Academy, an apothecary-themed version of the classic Game of Pig. The core mechanics of the dice game will remain the same, but the interface will be visually redesigned to feel like an old botanical workshop. Players will “brew” potions as they roll, accumulating points until they choose to pass or risk losing their turn if a one is rolled. Snake eyes will represent a shattered potion, resetting the player's total score.
