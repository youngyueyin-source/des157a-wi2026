A botanical twist on the classic game of Pig.

Welcome to
Apothecary Academy

First player to reach 30 points wins.
This game requires strategy and luck!

Reveal cards to accumulate points that brew your potion to perfection.

If you reveal a three-leaf clover, your turn ends. If you reveal two three-leaf clovers, your potion is destroyed by bad luck.

TIP: The four-leaf clover indicates which lucky player's turn it is.
